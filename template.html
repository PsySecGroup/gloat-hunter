Query Options

  • basket finds tweets that contain 'basket' (Like 'basketball and basketweaving)
  • basket OR glove will find tweets that contains 'basket' or 'glove'
  • basket AND glove will find tweets that contains 'basket' and 'glove'
  • "basket" will only find tweets with the exact word 'basket'
  • NOT basket will not return tweets that contain 'basket'
